Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 5, 2026 Brewlio is based in Australia.

What we collect

  • Quiz answers (brew method, grinder tier, taste preferences) to generate your result.
  • Basic usage events (e.g., view question, next/prev, completion timing) to improve Brewlio.
  • Email address only if you choose to submit it for updates or matched coffees.

We do not collect passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information via the quiz.

Why we collect it

  • To generate your coffee style match and show it to you.
  • To understand what’s working (or breaking) so we can improve the quiz.
  • If you provide your email: to send updates or coffee matches related to your profile.

How we store it

Brewlio uses a hosted database (Supabase) to store quiz events and (optionally) email signups. We store random identifiers for session/submission so we can connect events to one quiz run.

We only access this data to operate and improve Brewlio.

Cookies and local storage

Brewlio may use your browser’s localStorage or sessionStorage to keep a simple session/run ID so the quiz works reliably (e.g., timing and completion).

You can clear this at any time via your browser’s site data / storage settings.

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal data. We don’t share your email with third parties for marketing.

Data retention

We keep quiz event data as long as it’s useful for improving the product. If you submit an email, we keep it until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

Your choices and requests

  • You can complete the quiz without entering an email.
  • You can request deletion by emailing us.

Contact: hello@brewlio.com.au

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy as Brewlio evolves. We’ll post the latest version here and update the effective date above.
